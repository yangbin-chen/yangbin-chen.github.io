Intro

I am currently an assistant professor of the Department of Computing at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) , which is located in a beatiful and historic city - Suzhou, China. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Qing Li and Prof. Jianping Wang and my B.Eng. (major) in Automation and B.A. (minor) in Communication from University of Science and Technology of China.

My research interests lie in few-shot learning and spoken language processing. I am also interested in machine intelligence for healthcare and humanity.

In my opinion , a good researcher should study fundamental theories, develop elegant algorithms, solve tough problems, and achieve social good.
