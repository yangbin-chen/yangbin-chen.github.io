Intro

I am currently a researcher of FUBIAN Medical Technology which is located in a beatiful and historic city - Suzhou, China. Before joining FUBIAN, I was postdoctoral fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Qing Li and Prof. Jianping Wang and my B.Eng. (major) in Automation and B.A. (minor) in Communication from University of Science and Technology of China.

My research interests lie in few-shot learning and its applications in speech and language processing. Currently I am focusing on machine intelligence for healthcare and humanity.

In my opinion , a good researcher should study fundamental theories, develop elegant algorithms, solve tough problems, and achieve social good.
